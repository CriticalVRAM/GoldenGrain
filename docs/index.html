About us

In these days of mass production, it is rare to find a bakery that devotes itself to quality over quantity.

Enlivened by incredible crisp fixings, our little neighborhood pastry shop on a corner in downtown Chicago started making craftsman breads and naturally prepared desserts. Also, upon a little achievement, neighbors started to approach us for sandwiches made with that awesome bread, trailed by hand crafted soups and servings of mixed greens, and even made-to-arrange scramblers. Our visitors’ solicitations kept on moving us as our bread kitchen’s menu and business developed. You’ll see the stove’s open fire and feel the glow from our broilers working throughout the day. They’re the customs we set up years prior that we still enthusiastically pursue today.

Golden Grain bakery uses only quality ingredients. All of our products are made from scratch with the finest ingredients, providing you with products of the highest quality. Golden Grain bakery is the perfect spot to have breakfast, a brunch date, or afternoon meeting for coffee and pastries.
