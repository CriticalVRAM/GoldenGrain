About us

In these days of mass production, it is rare to find a bakery that devotes itself to quality over quantity.

Golden Grain bakery uses only quality ingredients. All of our products are made from scratch with the finest ingredients, providing you with products of the highest quality. Golden Grain bakery is the perfect spot to have breakfast, a brunch date, or afternoon meeting for coffee and pastries.
